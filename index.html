Welcome to RedVein Industries

RedVein Industries is a structured military-industrial organization built upon discipline, loyalty, operational efficiency, and command authority. Members of RedVein Industries are expected to maintain professionalism at all times while representing the organization.

“Discipline is not an option. It is our foundation.”

This page serves as the official public information hub for RedVein Industries. It provides an overview of the group’s structure, expectations, regulations, and rank hierarchy.

Notice: This page is maintained for informational and organizational use. Personnel must still follow official orders from Command, High Command, and authorized staff.

Mission Statement

The mission of RedVein Industries is to establish and preserve order through disciplined personnel, structured operations, and a reliable chain of command. The organization values loyalty, unity, professionalism, and operational strength.

Every member plays a role in maintaining the stability of RedVein Industries. From recruits to command personnel, each rank is expected to contribute to the continued growth and success of the organization.

General Regulations

Regulations exist to protect the structure and reputation of RedVein Industries. Violations may result in warnings, temporary prohibitions, removal from operations, or permanent prohibition depending on severity.

Core Expectations

  • Follow orders issued by superior personnel.
  • Maintain professionalism during all operations.
  • Do not disrupt official activities.
  • Do not abuse authority, tools, or permissions.
  • Do not harass, exploit, or intentionally damage group operations.
  • Respect the chain of command.

Disciplinary Articles

Article 1.1 — Negligence of Procedure

Failure to comply with established procedures or operational regulations is prohibited.

Article 1.2 — Unprofessional Conduct

Immature or disruptive behavior while representing RedVein Industries is prohibited.

Article 2.1 — False Accusations

Knowingly providing false reports or allegations against another member is prohibited.

Article 2.2 — Restricted Access

Entering unauthorized sectors or restricted zones without clearance is prohibited.

Article 3.1 — Unauthorized Homicide

Random killing, unauthorized lethal force, or unlawful hostile activity is prohibited.

Article 3.2 — Disrespect Toward Superior Personnel

Insulting or undermining superior ranking personnel is prohibited.
